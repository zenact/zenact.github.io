How Zenact unlocks your inner talents

1% better every day and the future

Every day there's obstacles and challenges. Frustrating as it may be to experience such delays in your progress on your goals, they can be a blessing.

If every day, we aim to get just 1% better, every soon we'll notice drastic changes. This can be difficult though, especially without the right framework to figure out how to best gain insight from the day. Zenact aims to tackle this problem using a very common and very powerful idea known as feedback loops.

Feedback Loops

Feedback loops are simply a tool used to measure and change something. They have wide spread use from the military to NASA to various scientific and engineering fields. Behind every problem or obstacle, there's things that came before. Figuring that out is one of our chief objectives. Armed with this knowledge, we can provide you with concrete ways to improve. When obstacles occur in the future, applying this same approach will help resolve them. Before you know it, it will seem like solving these issues was a piece of cake! You'll be many steps closer towards becoming your true self!
