How Zenact gamifies goal building and breaking

How exactly can goals be gamified?

It can be difficult to stay on track with goals. Life happens. Maybe we have a bad day or we're going through a rough patch. In those moments especially, making progress on our goals is extra satisfying but also feels more challenging.

Psychology from video games and other products such as Facebook or Reddit, show us the key elements we have adapted. With this, we work with you to figure out what your goals are and how to break them down into smaller subgoals. Next, we feed this data into our AI, and we're able to start the process of gamifying your goals!

Before you know it, you'll be looking forward to and automatically working on what's most important to you. We're excited to embark on this journey with you!
